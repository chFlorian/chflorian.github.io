Payments

Payments are handled by Apple. They are responsible for everything payment-related.

Refund Policy

You can refund your payment through your Apple Account. This is not handled by the developer of LaunchBuddy.

Subscriptions

Subscriptions automatically renew after the subscription period ends. You can cancel your subscription during a free trial without getting charged for the first period. You will only get charged after a potential free trial ends.

Contact

Get in contact through the feedback button in the LaunchBuddy settings.
