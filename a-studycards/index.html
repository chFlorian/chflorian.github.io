A+ Study Cards

A simple study card app designed by Florian Schweizer

Manage & Study your cards
on iPhone, iPad and Mac


Organising your cards

All your cards are stored inside a topic, e.g. CS 100. These topics are then grouped by your liking, e.g. by semesters or years.

Study

Just go into a topic and start studying, that's really it! My goal is to keep the application as simple as possible, while also incorporating all needed features.

Continue Anywhere

Your groups, topics and cards are all synchronized across your Apple devices through your A+ Study Cards account, so you can e.g. create all cards on your Mac and then study in the train on your iPhone.

Start your semester off right!

Get A+ Study Cards now on all of your iDevices.
