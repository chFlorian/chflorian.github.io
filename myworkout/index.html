Intuitive Icons

Simple and intuitive icons are available for all preinstalled workouts' tasks. This makes it super easy to get started with your fitness journey even without any prior experience.

Unique Achievements

Keep your motivation going by collecting all achievements. These include accumulated durations, repetitions (e.g. pushups) or streaks like working out every day for a week, month or even year straight.

Daily Schedule

Create a per-day weekly workout schedule perfectly fitting to your preferences. Once setting a time, you will receive notifications every day as a reminder to stay active.
